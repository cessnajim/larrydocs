Larry Video Publisher — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Overview

Larry Video Publisher ("the Service") is a personal content management tool used by a single creator. This privacy policy explains what data the Service handles.

2. Data We Collect

The Service does not collect any data from third-party users or viewers.

The only data processed by the Service belongs to the creator:

  • Video files stored in the creator's private media library
  • Platform API credentials (YouTube, TikTok) for publishing
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags) created by the Service
  • Publishing history and analytics retrieved from platform APIs

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on the creator's private server infrastructure. No data is stored on third-party cloud services or shared with external parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service connects to the following platforms via their official APIs:

  • YouTube — for video uploading and analytics retrieval
  • TikTok — for video uploading

Data shared with these platforms is limited to video content and metadata that the creator explicitly chooses to publish. Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle published content.

5. Data Sharing

The Service does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties. The Service does not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

6. Data Retention

Video files and metadata are retained on the creator's private server indefinitely or until manually deleted by the creator.
